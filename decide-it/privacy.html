Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Decide It ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed as a local-first application, meaning your data stays on your device.

2. Data Collection and Use

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to our own servers.

  • Custom decisions, lists, and history are stored locally on your device (using Hive).
  • App settings such as theme, sound, and haptic preferences are also stored locally.
  • No account, login, or personal data is required to use the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect limited data:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Google AdMob)

AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and device-related information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

We do not control how third-party services collect or use data.

4. Log Data

We do not collect log data or use analytics/crash reporting SDKs.

5. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Vibration -> for haptic feedback
  • Sensors -> for "Shake to Roll/Toss" functionality

All data from these features is processed in real-time on your device and is never stored or shared externally.

6. Data Deletion and Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Localized history data (including Coin Toss records, Group assignments, and Split Bills) is capped at the 15 most recent entries to ensure optimal app performance and minimize local storage usage.
  • You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us
