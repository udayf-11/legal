Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Decide It ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

2. Information Collection and Use

Decide It is designed as a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to our own servers.

  • Custom Decisions & History: All your entered data, custom lists, and decision history are stored strictly on your device using local storage (Hive).
  • Device Settings: Your theme preferences and sound/haptic settings are also stored locally.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob: We use AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your advertising ID and other non-personal identifiers to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

4. Log Data

In the event of an error or crash, the app may collect standard technical information (Log Data) to help us improve the service. This may include your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, and the time/date of your use.

5. Device Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback.
  • Sensors: To enable "Shake to Roll/Toss" functionality.

These data are processed in real-time on your device and are never shared.

6. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support.solverse@gmail.com
